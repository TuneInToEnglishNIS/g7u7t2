1. What is the central message of the song’s chorus, “Be proactive, you're in charge, Begin with the end in mind, Success will be LARGE”?
2. According to the song, why is it important to “put first things first” and “Work then Play”?
3. What does the phrase “Think Win-Win” mean in the song, and why is it encouraged “every single day”
4. How does the song suggest that respecting others on the bus and earning trust in a team are related?
5. According to the song's lyrics, why does having the “7 Habits” make you a leader for the rest of your life?

Suggested Answers

1. The central message is that taking control of your actions and goals, while planning for success, leads to significant achievements.
2. It’s important because prioritizing important tasks before leisure activities helps in achieving success and maintaining discipline.
3. “Think Win-Win” means seeking solutions that benefit all parties involved. It's encouraged daily to promote collaboration and positive outcomes.
4. Respecting others on the bus and earning trust in a team are related because they both involve showing consideration and building positive relationships.
5. Having the “7 Habits” is associated with leadership because they provide a framework for personal and interpersonal effectiveness, which can lead to leadership roles and success throughout life.
